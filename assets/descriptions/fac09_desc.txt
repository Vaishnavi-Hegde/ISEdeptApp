Prof. Arati S. Nayak Assistant Professor

About
Prof. Arati S. Nayak is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Information Science and Engineering at SDM College of Engineering and Technology, Dharwad, Karnataka, India. She obtained her Bachelor of Engineering from Karnatak University, Dharwad and a Master’s degree from VTU, Belagavi. She has guided several UG and PG projects.

Qualifications: B.E., M. Tech(CSE)

Experience: 10 Years
